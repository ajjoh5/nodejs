Adam Johnstone

Adam is a disruptive tech innovator, entrepreneur and business owner located in Melbourne, Australia

Driven, absolutely 110% to bring the latest in tech innovations, software and coding frameworks to entrepreneurs and companies worldwide to help them out innovate, out disrupt and oust their competition.

Why Tech Innovation?

There's a lot of tech disruptors out there - Elon Musk, Stewart Butterfield, Chris Wanstrath, Dev Ittycheria all with a wonderful cause.

Adam has worked as a IT and Tech consultant to some of the largest leading brands and government departments - NAB, ANZ, Telstra, Henley Homes, Dodo and noticed a common thread.

Adam observed that, even at the highest levels of business when solving a big problem not a lot of thought goes into the initial product selection, or the technical framework of choice.

As TedX talker Tai Lopez quotes - "Make war with a multitude of counsellors and allies" - and what Adam brings to the table is a vast wealth of knowledge of open source platforms, licensed platforms and the latest technologies collected from around the globe. 

These include some of the following:

  • NodeJS - a server side javascripting framework, non-blocking
  • Angular 1.x & 2 (beta) - a client side heroic stack for front end UI goodness
  • Microsoft SharePoint - intranets, extranets, document management and workflows for enterprise
  • Ionic - Hybrid application for native iPhone, iPad, Android Devices and Windows apps
  • jQuery - a client side javascript framework to bind javascript events to controls on page
  • Microsoft ASP.NET, MVC, C# - server side code frameworks to build enterprise applications

Before you set out to solve your problem and choose a technology stack, have someone like Adam as one of your counsellors, before you make war with your competition.

Adam's Background 

Adam started coding when he was 13 years old, after reading a series of Visual Basic 3.0 books cover-to-cover on a family holiday.

In high school he was teaching programming classes and in University lead the collaborative projects usually as Team Lead and Lead Developer. 

In the industry Adam worked for large IT consultancies before niching down into specific technology platforms to get a better understanding of all of the available products and coding frameworks available to startups, small businesses and enterprise.

What he realised is that in most cases, people are making decisions with less than half the picture.

Usually technology stacks, platforms and products are chosen purely because the company or the coder is 'familiar' and 'comfortable' with the product.

As a business owner, this means you're not looking at the full picture and making a good decision about the future.

Adam's Manifesto

Adam believes and supports Eric Ries's manifesto (outlined in the bestselling book the Lean Startup) of "Build, Measure, Learn" - but adds that a first step of "Select" is required to more carefully "select" the best idea / platform / stack before moving through the build, measure learn loop.
